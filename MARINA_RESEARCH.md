## Marina Companies in Turkey

### 1. Setur Marinas (seturmarinas.com)

**Type:** Large Marina Chain
**Number of Marinas:** ~14 marinas
**Management Model:** Multi-location marina chain
**Parent Company:** Koç Holding

#### Key Characteristics:
- One of the largest marina operators in Turkey
- Operates approximately 14 marinas across different locations
- Part of Koç Holding, one of Turkey's largest conglomerates
- Standardized operations and management systems
- Part of larger Setur tourism/travel group
- Operating marinas since 1998

#### Recent Major Development - Kalamış Marina:
**Contract Details (February 2025):**
- **40-year operating rights** for Fenerbahçe-Kalamış Marina
- **Contract Value:** $504 million USD
- **Payment Structure:**
  - $176.4 million (35%) paid at signing (February 2025)
  - Remaining $327.6 million in 5 equal installments over 60 months
- **Investment Requirement:** $150 million USD within 10 years
- **Quality Standard:** Must upgrade to 4-anchor certification within 10 years

**Timeline:**
- 1998: Setur becomes sole operator of Kalamış Marina
- 2021: First tender won by Koç Holding, later cancelled
- July 16, 2024: New tender - Vahit Karaaslan bids $505M, Koç bids $504M
- December 23, 2024: Karaaslan withdraws, forfeits deposit
- February 5, 2025: Koç Holding signs contract and takes over

#### Strategic Implications for Ada Maritime AI:
- **CRITICAL TIMING:** Fresh contract start = ideal for technology adoption
- **Major Investment:** $150M modernization budget = room for digital transformation
- **Quality Upgrade:** 4-anchor standard requirement = service excellence imperative
- **Large Corporate:** Koç Holding backing = sophisticated operations, decision-making power

**Use Cases for Kalamış Marina:**
  - AI-powered customer service during modernization phase
  - Multilingual support for premium clientele
  - 24/7 availability during construction/upgrade period
  - Knowledge base for new staff during transition
  - Standardized service quality during transformation
  - Documentation and compliance tracking for 4-anchor certification

**Broader Setur Network Opportunities:**
  - Standardized customer service across all 14 locations
  - Centralized booking and reservation management
  - Fleet-wide data analytics and optimization
  - Consistent service quality through AI assistance
  - Multi-location knowledge base for staff
  - Pilot at Kalamış → Scale to 14 marinas
